Cookies on Digital Marketplace

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

Digital Marketplace uses cookies that are essential for the site to work.

How cookies are used on Digital Marketplace

Session cookie

We store a session cookie on your device to help keep your information secure while you use the service.

Measuring website usage

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the Digital Marketplace so we can improve it based on user needs.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to Digital Marketplace
  • the pages you visit on Digital Marketplace and government digital services, and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you’re using the service

We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.
