Cookies on Digital Marketplace

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use the Digital Marketplace, such as the pages you visit.

How cookies are used on Digital Marketplace

Measuring website usage

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the Digital Marketplace so we can improve it based on user needs.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to Digital Marketplace
  • the pages you visit on Digital Marketplace and government digital services, and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you’re using the service

We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.

Name Purpose Expires
_ga Helps us count how many people visit Digital Marketplace by tracking if you’ve visited the service before 2 years
_gid Helps us count how many people visit Digital Marketplace by tracking if you’ve visited the service before 24 hours
_gat Used to manage the rate at which page view requests are made 10 minutes
_gat_govuk_shared Helps us count how many users visit [GOV.UK](http://www.gov.uk) after visiting Digital Marketplace 10 minutes

Introductory message cookie

When you first use Digital Marketplace, you see a message about how the site uses cookies and how to find out about them. Once you’ve seen the message, we store a cookie on your computer so it knows not to show it again.

Name Purpose Expires
seen_cookie_message Tells us that you have seen our cookie message 1 month

Accepting cookie settings

We test whether your browser is accepting cookies from Digital Marketplace. This allows us to give useful error messages when things go wrong.

Name Purpose Expires
dm_cookie_probe Tells us if your browser is accepting cookies 1 year

Session cookies

We store session cookies on your computer to help keep your information secure while you use the service.

Name Purpose Expires
dm_session Stores encrypted session data When you close your browser

User research recruitment banner cookie

When you use our service, you may see a pop up banner about user research recruitment. Once you’ve interacted with the message (by visiting or clicking on any of the links), we store a cookie on your computer so it knows not to show it again.

Name Purpose Expires
seen_user_research_message Tells us that you have seen our user research banner Never expires
