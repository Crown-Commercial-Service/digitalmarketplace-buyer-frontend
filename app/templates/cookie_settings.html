Cookies on Digital Marketplace

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use the Digital Marketplace, such as the pages you visit.

Cookie settings

We use Javascript to set most of our cookies. Unfortunately Javascript is not running on your browser, so you cannot change your settings. You can try:

  • reloading the page
  • turning on Javascript in your browser

We use 3 types of cookies. You can choose which cookies you’re happy for us to use.

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the Digital Marketplace so we can improve it based on user needs. Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to Digital Marketplace
  • the pages you visit on Digital Marketplace and government digital services, and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you’re using the service

We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.
