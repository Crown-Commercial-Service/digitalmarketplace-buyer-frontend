Before you start

  1. Prepare your requirements and set evaluation criteria.
  2. Get approval to buy what you need.

Create a shortlist

  1. Download the list of user research labs from the Digital Marketplace.
  2. Filter the list of labs on location and your requirements to create a shortlist.

Contact your shortlist

  1. Send your requirements and evaluation criteria to shortlisted suppliers.
  2. Answer supplier questions.
  3. Suppliers will tell you if they can meet your requirements.

Evaluate

  1. Evaluate suppliers responses on:
    • availability
    • location, facilities and accessibility
    • price
  2. Award a contract to the supplier that best meets your needs.
